About Me

Hello! My name is Ben. I'm a student at the Medill School of Journalism at Northwestern University in Evanston, Illinois, where I'm studying journalism and the mathematical methods in the social sciences.

If I could describe myself in three words, I would choose journalist, explorer, and mathematician. I love to write and tell stories, to travel to unexplored places with different cultures, and to crunch numbers and find derivatives. Am I left-brained or right-brained? Well, I guess you could say I'm a little bit of both.
