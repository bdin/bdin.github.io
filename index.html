Hello! I'm Ben.

Hi! My name is Benjamin Din, and I'm a journalism student at Northwestern University. I'm currently a reporting intern at The Star, a daily newspaper in Johannesburg. On campus, I'm a student fellow at the Knight Lab, where I explore the intersection of journalism and technology. I also work at The Daily Northwestern, where I report, write and code. Last summer, I was an interactive intern at the San Francisco Chronicle.

I like to write. I like to code. If it's not too much to ask, one day I hope to be doing both. But for now – while I'm here at Northwestern – I'll keep on doing what I do best: spending late nights in the newsroom, doing marathon coding sessions, solving crossword puzzles and counting down the days until I get to go back to sunny California.
